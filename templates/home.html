About Us

We are four students of the Master's in Bioinformatics for Health Sciences at UPF. This application was developed as part of our Databases and Web Development course, where we aimed to integrate bioinformatics and healthcare to create a tool that supports healthcare professionals in cancer treatment decisions.

OncoTrack is a platform designed to assist doctors and nurses in analyzing genetic data and providing personalized cancer treatment recommendations. By integrating cutting-edge bioinformatics techniques with a user-friendly interface, the app helps medical professionals make informed decisions tailored to patients' genetic profiles. Our mission is to bridge the gap between genomic data and clinical practice, making precision medicine more accessible and actionable.

Our Services

Our platform provides comprehensive services for oncological data analysis, making it easier to understand cancer-related mutations and find the best treatment options.

Variant Analysis

Our advanced algorithms analyze patient genetic data from VCF files, identifying key mutations that may be related to the patient's condition.

KEGG Enrichment Analysis

We perform pathway enrichment analysis to provide insights into the biological processes and pathways affected by the patient's genetic variants.

Mutational Signature Analysis

Discover mutational signatures that reveal underlying causes of the mutations, helping to understand the tumor's origin and potential responses to treatment.

Treatment Options & Resistances

OncoTrack displays the most relevant treatment options based on the patient's genetic makeup, including insights into potential resistances, helping doctors make informed decisions.

Guided Treatment for Nurses

Once the doctor selects a treatment, our system ensures nurses are informed and ready to administer the correct treatment, streamlining patient care and improving accuracy.
